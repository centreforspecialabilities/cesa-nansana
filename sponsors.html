For Businesses

Become a Corporate Sponsor

Great CSR isn't just a donation line item — it's a story worth telling. Partner with CeSA and share genuine, feel-good impact with your customers and team.

We work with a small number of companies — in Uganda and abroad — who want their support to be visible and meaningful, not just a line in a CSR report. When you sponsor CeSA, you get real, shareable content: photos, videos, and a genuine connection to the children your company is helping.

Sponsorship Packages

Ways to Partner With CeSA

Supporter

From $50 / month

Your company logo featured on the CeSA website, seen by families, donors and partners around the world.

Champion

From $200 / month

Everything in Supporter, plus: your company's sticker placed on one of the chairs your donation helped repair, and a personal thank-you video from the children.

In-Kind Partner

No fixed price

Sponsor us with what you already have — furniture, stationery, equipment, or professional services. We'll recognize your contribution just the same.

Exact packages and pricing are still being finalized — get in touch and we'll tailor a sponsorship that fits your company and budget.
