Where Our Graduates Are Now

CeSA doesn't stop at the classroom door. We're gathering stories from our graduates about the lives and careers they've built since leaving CeSA.

Graduate Story

Jireh M. Victor

Current age: 20

Years at CeSA: 5 years

Current job: Freelancer — content creator at one of the most recognized companies

How he got the job: Found it personally, on his own

Before CeSA

I have been in both ordinary school and special school.

At CeSA

I took part in all the activities there.

The best thing about CeSA

"The way they care — that I'm even out of school, but they still care about my well-being."

Favorite memory

"I really don't have a favorite memory, because they are all good memories."

His dream

"I don't really know — I'm a free person. I just want to be happy and be able to enjoy myself."

A message to parents

"I was once just like your kid. I know what you're thinking, because my parents thought it too — but with the right help, your child can become someone tomorrow, even better than me."
